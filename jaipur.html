Jaipur
I went to Jaipur , Rajasthan with my family and now I am giving a pinch of what I saw there . Jaipur , It is known as the pink city of India. I arrived at that place by rail. The Railway Station was very well architect with several Big wall paintings of Maharajas lived there. After coming out of the station , We boarded a taxi and went to my maternal uncle's home. Due to fatigue, we decided to travel the next day. On the next day in the morning, We boarded a bus and went to many historical sit es like Amber Fort and Palace , Hawa Mahal , City Palace, Nahargarh Fort , Jaigar Fort , Monkey temple and many other alluring places. Apart from the historical monuments, it has the offices of the embassies, high commissions, trading firms and many other organisations. Jaipur indeed, is a treasure-chest of antiquity and heart of India. After staying there for a couple of weeks. We all got back to our home through rail. It was great experience and it was nice place for knowing new things.
